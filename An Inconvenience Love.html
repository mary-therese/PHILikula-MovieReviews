AN INCONVENIENCE LOVE

RELEASED DATE: November 23, 2022

STARRING:   Belle Mariano & Donny Pangilinan

CAST:   Tirso Cruz, JC Alcantara, Jeffrey Quizon, Matet De Leon, Adrian Lindayag, Iana Bernandez, Sheenly Gener, Brian Sy, Chino Lui, Teresa Loyzaga, Nicco Manlo, Jomari Angeles, Clara Del Rosario, Dwein Baltazar, Aldo Razmjoo

GENRE:   Drama & Romance

RATINGS:   58% Critique Reviews, 99% Audience Reviews

DIRECTOR:  Petersen Vargas

AUTHOR:  Daisy Cayanan & Enrico C. Santos

OVERVIEW:

Ayef dreams of being an animator and has no time for love. When she meets Manny, they agree to a convenient relationship that will end the month she's set to leave for Singapore.

PalmBeachG

Really, the first time I ever watched a Filipino movie or drama. For some reason it came up on my Netflix list so I decided to watch it and found it was a nice love story. Actually I never heard of the term "Inconvenient Love" before so I didn't even know what they meant and they added it to their love contract that they made - what I took from it is that it meant that you only love when it is convenient for you - such as only when you are happy and not when the other person may need you the most because things are going sad or going wrong. You may want to bail when things get tough. Well the lead male I definitely loved in his role - he was cute and played it perfectly. Both the ML and the FL learned from their love so it was worth watching.
